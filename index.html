How to play

Each player has a hand of five dice. When it's your turn, you can roll the dice up to three times. Try to get the highest possible rank of dice before your turns are over!

Ranking of hands

  • RANK 0 - Nothing five mismatched dice forming no sequence longer than four.
  • RANK 1 - Pair two dice showing the same value.
  • RANK 2 - Two Pairs two pairs of dice, each showing the same value.
  • RANK 3 - Three-of-a-Kind three dice showing the same value.
  • RANK 4 - Five High Straight dice showing values from 1 through 5, inclusive.
  • RANK 5 - Six High Straight dice showing values from 2 through 6, inclusive.
  • RANK 6 - Full House Pair of one value and Three-of-a-Kind of another.
  • RANK 7 - Four-of-a-Kind four dice showing the same value.
  • RANK 8 - Five-of-a-Kind all five dice showing the same value.

Welcome to Dice Poker!

How to play

Each player has a hand of five dice. When it's your turn, you can roll the dice up to three times. After each turn you can click a dice to "hold" it. A dice which is being held will not be thrown on the next roll. Try to get the highest possible rank of dice before your turns are over!

Ranking of hands

  • RANK 0 - Nothing five mismatched dice forming no sequence longer than four.
  • RANK 1 - Pair two dice showing the same value.
  • RANK 2 - Two Pairs two pairs of dice, each showing the same value.
  • RANK 3 - Three-of-a-Kind three dice showing the same value.
  • RANK 4 - Five High Straight dice showing values from 1 through 5, inclusive.
  • RANK 5 - Six High Straight dice showing values from 2 through 6, inclusive.
  • RANK 6 - Full House Pair of one value and Three-of-a-Kind of another.
  • RANK 7 - Four-of-a-Kind four dice showing the same value.
  • RANK 8 - Five-of-a-Kind all five dice showing the same value.
